Pest Control Guide

Common Crop Diseases

Click on a disease to learn about its symptoms, prevention, and treatment.

🍅 Tomato Blight

Symptoms: Brown spots on leaves and fruits.

Prevention: Avoid overwatering and improve air circulation.

Treatment: Apply recommended fungicide.

🌾 Rice Blast

Symptoms: White or gray spots on leaves.

Prevention: Use disease-resistant rice varieties.

Treatment: Spray fungicide at the early stage.

🥔 Potato Late Blight

Symptoms: Dark brown patches on leaves.

Prevention: Keep the field clean and avoid excess moisture.

Treatment: Use copper-based fungicide.

🐛 Aphid Attack

Symptoms: Curled leaves and sticky sap.

Prevention: Inspect plants regularly.

Treatment: Use neem oil or insecticide.
